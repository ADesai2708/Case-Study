Indian States Travel Guide

Bihar

Popular Destinations:

  • Bodh Gaya: Mahabodhi Temple (UNESCO site)
  • Nalanda: Nalanda University ruins

Delhi

Popular Destinations:

  • Old Delhi:India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhavan, Akshardham Temple
  • New Delhi: Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Qutub Minar

West Bengal

Popular Destinations:

  • Kolkata: Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge

Rajasthan

Popular Destinations:

  • Jaipur: Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal (Pink City)
  • Jaisalmer: Golden Fort, Desert Safari

Odisha

Popular Destinations:

  • Puri: Jagannath Temple, Puri Beach
  • Konark: Sun Temple (UNESCO site)
  • Bhubaneswar:Lingaraj Temple, Udayagiri Caves

Himachal Pradesh

Popular Destinations:

  • Shimla:Mall Road,Ridge

Tamil Nadu

Popular Destinations:

  • Chennai: Kapaleeshwarar Temple, Fort St. George, Government Museum, Marina Beach
  • Madurai: Meenakshi Amman Temple, Thirumalai Nayakar Mahal, Vaigai Dam
  • Mahabalipuram: Shore Temple, Mahabalipuram Beach, Five Rathas
  • Ooty: Scenic hill station with botanical gardens and lakes

Uttar Pradesh

Popular Destinations:

  • Agra: Taj Mahal, Agra Fort
  • Varanasi: Ghats along the Ganges, Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  • Lucknow: Bara Imambara, Rumi Darwaza
  • Fatehpur Sikri: Historical Mughal city with palaces and forts

Gujarat

Popular Destinations:

  • Statue of Unity: World's tallest statue honoring Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel
  • Ahmedabad: Sabarmati Ashram, Sidi Saiyyed Mosque
  • Somnath: Somnath Temple
  • Rann of Kutch: Vast salt desert

Haryana

Popular Destinations:

  • Kurukshetra: Brahma Sarovar, Jyotisar, Panorama Museum
  • Gurgaon: Modern city with corporate hubs and malls
  • Pinjore Gardens: Beautiful Mughal garden
  • Sultanpur National Park: Bird sanctuary

Jharkhand

Popular Destinations:

  • Ranchi: Dassam Falls, Birsa Zoological Park
  • Jamshedpur: Jubilee Park, Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Deoghar: Baidyanath Temple, Naulakha Mandir
  • Netarhat: Hill station known for sunrise and sunset views

Karnataka

Popular Destinations:

  • Bangalore: Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Bangalore Palace
  • Mysore: Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill
  • Hampi: Virupaksha Temple, Stone Chariot
  • Coorg: Abbey Falls, Talakaveri

Kerala

Popular Destinations:

  • Alleppey: Backwaters, Houseboats
  • Munnar: Tea Plantations, Eravikulam National Park
  • Kochi: Fort Kochi, Chinese Fishing Nets
  • Wayanad: Edakkal Caves, Banasura Sagar Dam
  • Kovalam: Lighthouse Beach, Hawah Beach

Madhya Pradesh

Popular Destinations:

  • Khajuraho: Khajuraho Group of Monuments
  • Bhopal: Upper Lake, Bhimbetka Rock Shelters
  • Ujjain: Mahakaleshwar Temple
  • Gwalior: Gwalior Fort, Jai Vilas Palace
  • Orchha: Orchha Fort Complex, Chaturbhuj Temple

Maharashtra

Popular Destinations:

  • Mumbai: Gateway of India, Marine Drive
  • Pune: Aga Khan Palace, Shaniwar Wada
  • Aurangabad: Ajanta and Ellora Caves
  • Lonavala: Bhushi Dam, Karla Caves
  • Mahabaleshwar: Venna Lake, Pratapgad Fort

Manipur

Popular Destinations:

  • Imphal: Kangla Fort, Ima Keithel (Women's Market)

  • Ukhrul: Shirui Lily Peak
  • Bishnupur: Keibul Lamjao National Park

Meghalaya

Popular Destinations:

  • Shillong: Ward's Lake, Don Bosco Museum
  • Cherrapunji: Living Root Bridges, Nohkalikai Falls

  • Jowai: Krang Suri Falls

Mizoram

Popular Destinations:

  • Aizawl: Mizoram State Museum, Durtlang Hills
  • Champhai: Rih Dil Lake, Lengteng Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Lunglei: Khawnglung Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Serchhip: Vantawng Falls
  • Thenzawl: Hmuifang Mountain

Nagaland

Popular Destinations:

  • Kohima: Kohima War Cemetery, Naga Heritage Village
  • Dimapur: Kachari Ruins, Rangapahar Reserve Forest
  • Mon: Longwa Village, Veda Peak
  • Mokokchung: Langpangkong Caves
  • Dzukou Valley: Trekking and camping

Punjab

Popular Destinations:

  • Amritsar: Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh
  • Chandigarh: Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake
  • Ludhiana: Punjab Agricultural University Museum
  • Patiala: Qila Mubarak, Sheesh Mahal
  • Anandpur Sahib: Virasat-e-Khalsa

Arunachal Pradesh

Popular Destinations:

  • Tawang: Tawang Monastery, Sela Pass
  • Itanagar: Ita Fort, Ganga Lake
  • Ziro: Ziro Valley, Talley Valley Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Bomdila: Bomdila Monastery, Apple Orchards
  • Dirang: Hot Water Springs, Sangti Valley

Goa

Popular Destinations:

  • Palolem Beach: Relaxation and water sports
  • Basilica of Bom Jesus: UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Dudhsagar Waterfalls: Scenic waterfalls amidst lush greenery
  • Anjuna Flea Market: Vibrant shopping experience
  • Chapora Fort: Panoramic views of the Arabian Sea

Uttarakhand

Popular Destinations:

  • Nainital: Naini Lake, Snow View Point
  • Mussoorie: Kempty Falls, Gun Hill Point
  • Kedarnath: Kedarnath Temple, Trekking trails
  • Auli: Skiing and cable car rides
  • Rishikesh: Laxman Jhula, River Rafting

Tripura

Popular Destinations:

  • Agartala: Ujjayanta Palace, Jagannath Temple
  • Unakoti: Ancient rock carvings and sculptures
  • Mata Tripureswari Temple: One of the 51 Shakti Peethas
  • Neer Mahal Palace: Lake palace on Rudrasagar Lake
  • Jampui Hills: Scenic hill station with orange plantations

Telangana

Popular Destinations:

  • Hyderabad: Charminar, Golconda Fort, Ramoji Film City
  • Nagarjuna Sagar Dam: Scenic dam with boating facilities
  • Bhadrachalam: Sri Sita Ramachandra Swamy Temple
  • Kuntala Waterfalls: Highest waterfall in Telangana
  • Nizamabad: Pocharam Wildlife Sanctuary, Nizamabad Fort

Sikkim

Popular Destinations:

  • Gangtok: MG Marg, Rumtek Monastery, Tashi Viewpoint
  • Tsomgo Lake: Glacial lake surrounded by mountains
  • Pelling: Pemayangtse Monastery, Rabdentse Ruins
  • Lachung: Yumthang Valley, Zero Point
  • Nathula Pass: Indo-China border pass with stunning views
